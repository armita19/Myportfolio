My Photography

From a very young age, I realised I had a passion for photography, which lead to my dad putting me into a photorgaphy workshop when we used to live in Dubai, UAE. when I moved and got older, my passion grew slightly dim until i inherited my late uncle's analogue zenit camera which coincidentally contained pictures of me from over 20 years ago! I took it as a sign from God and ever since, I now carry a camera ( I now have 6 different cameras xD) with me everywhere in case I find a good shot worth taking, I truly love photography especially since I constanty find beauty in the smallest things and cameras provide me with the opportunity to keep them forever.

If you're interested in seeing more of my photography, you can support me by following/viewing my instagram!
