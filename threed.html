Toaster Concept

This is an assignment of mine to make a unique toaster. This concept rethinks the toaster as a toaster container. Bread is placed into the top section, drops into the toaster body, and after toasting it exits from the bottom onto a plate underneath. The design explores gravity, flow, containment, and serving as part of a university assignment.
